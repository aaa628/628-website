PROJECT

pj_0 (Project Zero)

Naruto-inspired Open World Ninja RPG

Core Concept

  • Massive open-world multiplayer RPG
  • Choose a ninja village, master chakra, build your legend
  • Player-driven progression and discovery

Villages & Map

  • 5 villages: Leaf, Sand, Mist, Cloud, Stone
  • Unique buffs & missions per village
  • Travel between biomes and landmarks

Combat & Chakra

  • Health/chakra bars: manage your energy
  • Upgradeable combat and jutsu mastery
  • Unlock new chakra natures as you level up

Clans & Kekkei Genkai

  • Pick a clan for buffs & unique powers
  • Sharingan, Byakugan, Rinnegan (admin)
  • Clan-exclusive abilities & items

Progression & Bounty

  • Max level: 1628
  • Bingo Book (rogue) & Ninja Merit (good)
  • Bounty/rankings unlock buffs

Missions & Exploration

  • Village-exclusive missions
  • Story-based and open-world quests
  • Unlocks for jutsus, items, secrets

Controls & Features

  • Blocking, dodging, melee, weapons
  • Ninjutsu, Taijutsu, Genjutsu
  • Chakra bar determines ability use

Upgrades & Future

  • XP-based leveling for all
  • More clans, expansions, events
  • PvP tournaments & admin powers
